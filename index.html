Instagram Templates

Fun templates to use in your Instagram Stories

Step 1

Search for Nichole Ciotti in the Instagram app.

Step 2

Tap TEMPLATES at the top of Nichole's profile.

Step 3

Tap through to the templates. Hold down on a blank template and screenshot to save.

Step 4

Start a new clip in your story, tap the camera roll button, and select one of the templates.

Step 5

Fill out the template, then send to your story!
